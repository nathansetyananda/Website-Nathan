I graduated from Loyola College High School in Semarang, one of the best high schools in Central Java and Indonesia. Later, I pursued my higher education at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) in Surabaya, majoring in Business Management. ITS is recognized as one of Indonesia’s leading institutes, renowned for its excellence in technology and business education.

Beyond my studies in Indonesia, I had the incredible opportunity to participate in a study exchange program at Shibaura Institute of Technology in Japan, which broadened my global perspective and deepened my understanding of international business and innovation.
